Welcome To Our Bookstore & Cafe

Contained within an old haunted hotel, and over four floors, our Book store and cafe is filled with hidden corridors and cosy reading nooks for you to enjoy the magic of stories. If you love old books like us, come on over and enjoy your next read, either by the roaring fireplace or up in the secret attic.

OPEN 10AM-2AM Daily
